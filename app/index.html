ABOUT

PZO - Podolsk Equipment Plant, produces equipment for polymer processing, as well as other auxiliary equipment for line automation.

We are a company of young and energetic professionals. Our entire team of designers, engineers, foremen and department heads is working for you. Our employees are always improving and confirming their qualifications. The plant has existed since 2016. During this time, more than 1000 pieces of equipment have been produced, such as hydraulic presses, crushers, centrifuges, washing complexes, peripheral equipment and much more.

We are constantly in contact with our customers, provide warranty and post-warranty services, modernize and automate our models at the request of the client. We offer complete turnkey solutions for business in the field of polymer waste processing. Installation of a turnkey line by our specialists takes no more than a day. We are always in touch and you will never be left alone with your problems. Our equipment is always and with pleasure will be demonstrated by our clients in their production.
